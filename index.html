About Me

I am a Physics graduate (Science Education) from the Federal Institute of São Paulo and currently a master’s student at the Institute of Physics, University of São Paulo. My research focuses on luminescence mechanisms in minerals, with applications in radiation dosimetry and dating. My master’s thesis investigates phototransfer and thermally assisted mechanisms in potassium feldspars.
